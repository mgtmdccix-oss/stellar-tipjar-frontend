# Video Script: Sending Your First Tip

**Duration:** 2 minutes  
**Target Audience:** First-time supporters  
**Platform:** YouTube, website tutorial

---

## Scene 1: Introduction (0:00-0:15)

**Visual:** Bright, welcoming title screen with Stellar Tip Jar logo

**Voiceover:**
"Welcome to Stellar Tip Jar! In this tutorial, I'm going to show you how to send your first tip to a creator in just a few simple steps. Whether you're supporting an artist, musician, developer, or any creator you love, this guide will walk you through the entire process."

**Visual:** Animated icons of different types of creators (artist, musician, developer, writer)

---

## Scene 2: Before You Start (0:15-0:30)

**Visual:** Checklist appearing on screen

**Voiceover:**
"Before we begin, make sure you have three things:
1. A Stellar wallet - we recommend Freighter
2. Some XLM (Stellar Lumens) - the cryptocurrency used for tips
3. Access to a creator's profile on Stellar Tip Jar

If you don't have a wallet yet, check out our separate guide on wallet setup. Once you're ready, let's dive in!"

**Visual:** Show Freighter extension icon, XLM coin animation, creator profile screenshot

---

## Scene 3: Finding a Creator (0:30-0:50)

**Visual:** Screen recording of Stellar Tip Jar website navigation

**Voiceover:**
"First, let's find a creator to support. There are a few ways to do this:

Option 1: Visit the Explore page to browse featured creators and categories.

Option 2: Use the search bar to find a specific creator by name.

Option 3: If a creator shared their Tip Jar link on social media, click that directly."

**On-screen text:** "Explore Page" → "Search Bar" → "Direct Link"

**Visual:** Demonstrate each method with screen recording

---

## Scene 4: Opening Creator Profile (0:50-1:10)

**Visual:** Click on creator profile, profile loads

**Voiceover:**
"Once you've found a creator you want to support, click on their profile. You'll see their profile information, a description of what they do, and most importantly - the tip input field."

**Visual:** Highlight different sections of profile:
- Profile picture
- Bio/description
- Tip history
- Tip input form

**On-screen text:** Circle key areas as voiceover mentions them

---

## Scene 5: Entering Tip Amount (1:10-1:25)

**Visual:** Mouse clicking on tip amount field, typing amount

**Voiceover:**
"Now for the exciting part - sending your tip! Click on the amount field and enter how much XLM you want to send. Even a small amount means a lot to creators.

Below the amount, you'll see the total cost including fees. And if you'd like, you can add a personal message to let the creator know you appreciate their work!"

**Visual:** 
- Click amount field
- Type "5" 
- Show fee breakdown
- Add message (optional)

**On-screen text:**
"Tip Amount: 5 XLM"  
"Network Fee: ~0.00001 XLM"  
"Platform Fee: ~0.25 XLM"  
"Total: ~5.25 XLM"

---

## Scene 6: Sending the Tip (1:25-1:45)

**Visual:** Click "Send Tip" button, Freighter popup appears

**Voiceover:**
"Everything looks good? Click the 'Send Tip' button. Your Freighter wallet will pop up asking you to confirm the transaction. This is a security feature to make sure you really want to send this tip.

Take a moment to review the details:
- The creator's address
- The tip amount
- Any message you added

Once you're sure everything is correct, click 'Approve' in your wallet."

**Visual:**
- Click "Send Tip" button
- Freighter popup appears
- Show transaction details highlighted
- Click "Approve"

**On-screen text:**
"Step 1: Review Details"  
"Step 2: Click 'Approve'"

---

## Scene 7: Confirmation (1:45-2:00)

**Visual:** Success message appears, tip shows in creator's tip history

**Voiceover:**
"And that's it! Your tip has been sent successfully. The Stellar network is extremely fast, so the creator receives it instantly.

You'll see a success message, and your tip will appear in the creator's tip history. They'll be notified of your support, and if you included a message, they'll see it too.

Thank you for supporting the creators you love!"

**Visual:**
- Success checkmark animation
- Tip appears in tip history
- Creator's tip counter increases
- Celebratory animation

**On-screen text:**
"✓ Tip Sent Successfully!"  
"Creator Received: 5 XLM"

---

## Scene 8: Next Steps (2:00-2:00)

**Visual:** Information cards/buttons appearing

**Voiceover:**
"Want to learn more? Check out our other guides:
- How to set up your wallet
- How to become a creator
- Tips for security and safety

Subscribe for more Stellar Tip Jar tutorials!"

**Visual:** Links to other documentation

---

## Production Notes

**Background Music:**
- Upbeat, positive tone
- Royalty-free recommended
- Fade in at 0:00-0:05, fade out at 1:55-2:00

**Sound Effects:**
- Click sounds for button presses
- Gentle "whoosh" for transitions
- Success sound for final confirmation

**Color Grading:**
- Bright and friendly
- Match Stellar Tip Jar brand colors
- High contrast for readability

**Pacing:**
- Clear, measured voiceover (140 WPM)
- Short pauses between sections
- Enough time to read on-screen text

**Captions:**
- Full captions for accessibility
- Highlight important terms
- Include timestamps
