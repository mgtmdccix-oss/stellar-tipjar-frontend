# Video Script: Setting Up Your Wallet

**Duration:** 3 minutes  
**Target Audience:** Complete beginners, non-technical users  
**Platform:** YouTube, website tutorial

---

## Scene 1: Introduction (0:00-0:20)

**Visual:** Title screen "Setting Up Your Stellar Wallet"

**Voiceover:**
"Before you can use Stellar Tip Jar, you need a wallet to hold your cryptocurrency. Don't worry - this is easier than it sounds! In this video, I'll walk you through setting up Freighter, the easiest wallet for beginners.

By the end of this video, you'll be ready to send and receive tips."

**Visual:** Show Freighter logo, animated wallet icon

---

## Scene 2: What is a Wallet? (0:20-0:40)

**Visual:** Animation showing wallet concept

**Voiceover:**
"Think of a wallet like a digital bank account. It stores your cryptocurrency and lets you send and receive money. Unlike a bank, no company controls it - only you have access to it.

You'll have:
- A public address (safe to share, like a bank account number)
- A recovery phrase (secret - like your password, but even more important)
- A personal password (just for your wallet)"

**Visual:** Label and highlight each component

**On-screen text:**
"Public Address: Shareable"  
"Recovery Phrase: Top Secret"  
"Password: Keep Secure"

---

## Scene 3: Installing Freighter (0:40-1:15)

**Visual:** Screen recording of installation process

**Voiceover:**
"Let's get started! First, we need to install the Freighter extension. I'll be using Chrome, but Freighter works on Firefox and other browsers too.

1. Open your web browser
2. Go to freighter.app
3. Click 'Add to Chrome'
4. Click 'Add Extension' when the popup appears
5. You'll see the Freighter icon appear in your browser toolbar"

**Visual:** Show each step with screen recording

**On-screen text:** Appear after each step number

---

## Scene 4: Creating Your Wallet (1:15-2:00)

**Visual:** Screen recording of wallet creation process

**Voiceover:**
"Now let's create your wallet! Click the Freighter icon in your browser toolbar.

You'll see a welcome screen. Click 'Create New Wallet' to start.

Next, Freighter will show you your recovery phrase - 12 special words. This is SUPER important. Write these words down on paper RIGHT NOW and store it somewhere safe. If you lose access to your wallet, this phrase is the only way to get your funds back.

Freighter will ask you to re-enter some of these words to make sure you wrote them down correctly. Do this carefully!"

**Visual:**
- Click extension icon
- Show welcome screen with "Create New Wallet" button
- Show recovery phrase (blurred for security)
- Writing it down animation
- Entering words back

**On-screen text:**
"⚠️ WRITE DOWN YOUR RECOVERY PHRASE"  
"NEVER SHARE WITH ANYONE"

---

## Scene 5: Set Your Password (2:00-2:20)

**Visual:** Password entry screen

**Voiceover:**
"Now create a strong password for your wallet. This should be something you'll remember, but something others can't guess.

A strong password has:
- Uppercase and lowercase letters
- Numbers and symbols
- At least 12 characters
- Nothing personal like your birthday"

**Visual:** Show password requirements appearing

**On-screen text:**
"Good password: Blue$Crypto#Moon2024"  
"Bad password: password123"

This address is your public address. It's safe to share with anyone who wants to send you money. It's like your bank account number.

You also see your balance, which is currently zero."

**Visual:** Show wallet dashboard, highlight address and balance

**On-screen text:**

"GQWJHG5QTFYWDF..."

## Scene 7: Funding Your Wallet (2:45-3:10)

**Visual:** Step-by-step animation or screen recording

**Voiceover:**
"Now you need to add some XLM to your wallet so you can send tips.

1. Go to a cryptocurrency exchange like Kraken or Coinbase
2. Buy some XLM with your regular money
3. Copy your Freighter wallet address
4. Withdraw your XLM to that address

That's it! You're all set to start using Stellar Tip Jar."

**Visual:** Show each step with screen recording or animation

**On-screen text:** Number each step

## Scene 8: Security Reminder (3:10-3:25)

**Visual:** Security checklist animation

**Voiceover:**
"Before you go, remember these security tips:

1. Never share your recovery phrase with anyone
2. Back it up in a safe place
3. Use a strong, unique password
4. Don't lend your computer to others
5. Keep your software updated

Follow these tips and your crypto will stay safe!"

## Production Notes

**Visual Effects:**
- Clear, bright interface
- Zoom in on important areas
- Smooth transitions between steps
- Use arrows to direct attention

**Animation:**
- Animated icons for concept explanation (Scene 2)
- Actual screen recording for installation
- Checkperson marks for security tips
- Clear highlighting of important elements

**Audio:**
- Clear, friendly voiceover (140 WPM)
- Calm background music throughout
- Click/notification sounds for UI interactions
- Slightly louder voiceover when emphasizing security

**Pacing:**
- Slow enough for beginners to follow
- Pause after instructions for viewers to follow along
- Repeat key points (especially recovery phrase importance)

**Captions:**
- Full captions for accessibility
- Highlight critical phrases like "RECOVERY PHRASE"
- Include on-screen text in captions

- Note that this is for entertainment/educational purposes
- Users should do their own research
- Not financial advice
