# How to Receive Tips as a Creator

This guide helps creators get set up and start receiving tips from supporters on Stellar Tip Jar.

## Prerequisites

To receive tips, you'll need:
- A Freighter wallet (or compatible wallet)
- A Stellar Tip Jar creator account
- Your profile set up with basic information

If you're just starting out, see [GETTING_STARTED.md](GETTING_STARTED.md).

---

## Setting Up Your Creator Profile

### Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

1. **Visit Stellar Tip Jar**
   - Go to the main Stellar Tip Jar website

2. **Click "Sign In" or "Create Account"**
   - Click the sign-in button
   - You'll see wallet connection options

3. **Select Your Wallet**
   - Choose "Freighter" or your wallet type
   - A connection dialog will appear

4. **Approve Connection in Your Wallet**
   - Your wallet extension will pop up
   - Review the connection request
   - Click "Approve" or "Connect"
   - You're now signed in!

### Step 2: Create Your Profile

1. **Go to Profile Settings**
   - Click on your account/profile menu
   - Look for "Creator Profile" or "Settings"

2. **Choose Your Username**
   - This is your unique profile path
   - Example: `tipjar.com/username`
   - Keep it simple, memorable, and relevant
   - Lowercase letters and numbers work best
   - Avoid special characters if possible

3. **Add a Profile Picture**
   - Click "Upload Photo" or similar
   - Choose a clear, professional image
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
   - Recommended size: 400x400px or larger
   - Tips: Use a headshot or logo, good lighting helps

4. **Write Your Bio**
   - Add 1-2 sentences describing what you do
   - Examples:
     - "Digital artist creating fantasy commission work"
     - "Indie musician - electronic & ambient"
     - "Software developer and open-source contributor"
   - Keep it concise (100-200 characters)

5. **Add Links**
   - Portfolio website or personal site
   - Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, etc.
   - Streaming platform (Twitch, etc.)
   - GitHub, Patreon, or other platforms

6. **Review and Save**
   - Check everything looks good
   - Click "Save Profile"
   - Your profile is now live!

## Sharing Your Profile

Now let's get supporters to find you!

### Copy Your Profile Link

1. **Find Your Profile URL**
   - Go to your profile page
   - Copy the URL from your address bar
   - Example: `https://tipjar.com/myusername`

2. **Test the Link**
   - Open it in a new incognito window
   - Make sure it's public
   - Verify everything displays correctly

### Share on Social Media

**Social media tips:**

1. **Twitter / X**
   - Tweet: "Support my work! Send me a tip on Stellar Tip Jar [link]"
   - Add to your bio
   - Pin a tweet with your link
   - Include in relevant threads

2. **Instagram**
   - Add to your bio section
   - Mention in Stories with sticker
   - Include in post captions
   - Share in your Highlights

3. **YouTube**
   - Add to channel description
   - Mention in video descriptions
   - Add as a card or end screen
   - Include in community posts

4. **TikTok**
   - Add to your bio
   - Mention in videos
   - Use as a call-to-action

5. **Discord**
   - Add to your server description
   - Create a tips channel
   - Include in welcome messages
   - Pin to relevant channels

6. **Streaming (Twitch, etc.)**
   - Add to panels below stream
   - Mention during streams
   - Add to alerts/overlays
   - Post in chat commands

### Share on Your Website

1. **If You Have a Website**
   - Add a "Support Me" or "Tips" section
   - Include a prominent button/link
   - Add a short description
   - Place it on your landing page

2. **Blog Integration**
   - Add to your blog post sidebars
   - Include at the end of articles
   - Mention in relevant posts

3. **Email Signature**
   - Add to your email footer
   - Help email subscribers find you

### Tell People Why

**Encourage tips by explaining:**
- What you use tips for (supplies, equipment, time)
- How tips help you create
- What supporters get (satisfaction of helping, message response, etc.)

Example:
"Tips help me purchase art supplies and dedicate time to creating new work. Every tip fuels my creativity!"

## Receiving and Managing Tips

### Seeing Tips Come In

1. **Dashboard**
   - Sign in to Stellar Tip Jar
   - Go to your Dashboard
   - You'll see recent tips in real-time

2. **Get Notified**
   - Each tip appears immediately
   - See supporter name and amount
   - Read any messages they included
   - Some platforms send email notifications

### Viewing Tip History

1. **Access Tip History**
   - Click "Tip History" or "All Tips"
   - See all tips you've received
   - Sorted by most recent first
   - Includes date, amount, and message

2. **Analytics**
   - View total tips received
   - See tips over time/week/month
   - Identify your top supporters
   - Track earnings trends

### Responding to Supporters

While optional, acknowledging tips is appreciated:

1. **Leave a Message (if available)**
   - Some creators can respond to tip messages
   - Thank supporters personally
   - Keep it brief and genuine

2. **Social Media Shout-Outs**
   - Tweet thanks to supporters
   - Mention them in Stories
   - Create a "supporter spotlight"
   - Show appreciation publicly

3. **Special Recognition**
   - For large tips, consider extra thanks
   - Feature supporters in your work
   - Give them a special credit
   - Create a "Supporters" page

## Withdrawing Your Tips

When you're ready, you can withdraw your tips to your wallet:

### How to Withdraw

1. **Go to Withdraw Page**
   - Sign in to your account
   - Click "Withdraw" or "My Tips"
   - You'll see your balance

2. **Check Your Balance**
   - See total XLM available
   - Confirm the amount looks right
   - Check your tip history if unsure

3. **Click "Withdraw All" or "Withdraw"**
   - The button might be slightly different
   - A confirmation dialog appears

4. **Confirm the Transaction**
   - Verify the amount
   - Look at the destination (should be your wallet)
   - Click "Confirm" when ready

5. **Freighter Approval**
   - Your wallet will pop up
   - Review the transaction
   - Click "Approve" or "Sign"
   - Your tips are being transferred

### Waiting for Withdrawal

1. **Processing Time**
   - Usually 5-20 seconds
   - Rarely takes more than 1 minute
   - Check Freighter to see transaction

2. **Confirmation**
   - See success message on website
   - Tips appear in your Freighter wallet
   - You now control the XLM

3. **Converting to Cash**
   - If you want to cash out:
   - Sign in to your exchange (Kraken, Coinbase, etc.)
   - Deposit your XLM
   - Sell XLM for your preferred currency
   - Withdraw to your bank account

## Troubleshooting

### Tips Show on Profile But Wallet Didn't Receive

**Possible causes:**
- Withdrawal hasn't confirmed (wait 1 min)
- Wrong wallet address connected
- Network issues (unlikely but possible)

**Solution:**
- Wait 2-3 minutes
- Refresh Freighter
- Check transaction history in Freighter
- Contact support with your username

### Profile Won't Display Tips

**Possible causes:**
- Profile not published
- Settings set to private
- Connection issue

**Solution:**
- Check profile privacy settings
- Ensure profile is "Public"
- Refresh the page
- Try in an incognito window
- Sign out and back in

### Can't Withdraw Tips

**Possible causes:**
- Insufficient balance
- Wallet not connected
- Network issue

**Solution:**
- Verify wallet is connected
- Check your balance
- Refresh the page
- Try reconnecting wallet
- Check your internet connection

### Profile Link Invalid

**Possible causes:**
- Wrong username
- Profile not published
- Site technical issue

**Solution:**
- Copy fresh link from your profile
- Check username spelling
- Ensure profile is public
- Test on different device/browser
