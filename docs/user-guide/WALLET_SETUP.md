# Setting Up Your Stellar Wallet

A wallet is essential for using Stellar Tip Jar. This guide will help you set up your first wallet and understand how to keep it secure.

## Why Do I Need a Wallet?

A wallet stores your cryptocurrency and allows you to:
- Send tips to creators
- Receive tips as a creator
- Manage your funds securely
- Control your own crypto (no middleman)

Think of it like a digital bank account that only you can access.

---

## Recommended: Freighter Wallet

Freighter is the most popular and user-friendly Stellar wallet. We recommend it for most users.

### Installation

**For Chrome:**
1. Visit [freighter.app](https://freighter.app)
2. Click "Add to Chrome"
3. Click "Add Extension" in the popup
4. You'll see the Freighter icon in your browser toolbar

**For Firefox:**
1. Visit [freighter.app](https://freighter.app)
2. Click "Add to Firefox"
3. Click "Add" in the popup
4. You'll see the Freighter icon in your browser toolbar

**For Other Browsers:**
- Check [freighter.app](https://freighter.app) for your browser's availability

### Creating a New Wallet

1. **Open Freighter**
   - Click the Freighter icon in your browser toolbar
   - You'll see the welcome screen

2. **Select "Create New Wallet"**
   - Click the "Create New Wallet" button
   - This creates a brand new wallet just for you

3. **Store Your Recovery Phrase Safely**
   - Freighter will show you a 12-word recovery phrase
   - **IMPORTANT:** Write these words down on paper and store somewhere safe
   - **Never share your recovery phrase with anyone**
   - This is the only way to recover your wallet if you lose access

4. **Confirm Your Recovery Phrase**
   - Freighter will ask you to re-enter some words from your phrase
   - This ensures you wrote it down correctly
   - Read carefully and enter the correct words

5. **Set a Strong Password**
   - Create a unique password for your wallet
   - Use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
   - Don't use personal information (birthdate, names, etc.)
   - Store your password securely

6. **Verify Your Setup**
   - You should now see your wallet dashboard
   - You have one account ready to use
   - You can see your public wallet address (starts with 'G')

### Funding Your Wallet

Now you need to add XLM (Stellar Lumens) to your wallet:

1. **Copy Your Wallet Address**
   - In Freighter, click on your account
   - Click the "Copy" button next to your public address
   - It looks like: `GXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX`

2. **Buy XLM on a Cryptocurrency Exchange**
   - Popular exchanges: Kraken, Coinbase, Binance, Uphold
   - Purchase the amount of XLM you want to send as tips
   - You'll need at least 1 XLM minimum balance in Stellar

3. **Withdraw to Your Wallet Address**
   - Go to the exchange's withdrawal page
   - Paste your wallet address (the one you copied)
   - Enter the amount of XLM to withdraw
   - Confirm the withdrawal

4. **Wait for Confirmation**
   - Stellar network is very fast
   - Usually 5-10 seconds, rarely up to a minute
   - You should see the XLM appear in your Freighter wallet

### Security Tips

**Protect Your Recovery Phrase:**
- Write it down on paper (not digital)
- Store it in a safe place (safe, vault, etc.)
- Never photograph or email it
- Never share it with anyone, even support staff

**Use a Strong Password:**
- Create a unique password you haven't used elsewhere
- Don't use: birthdate, pet names, "password123"
- Consider using a password manager to store it securely
- Change your password regularly

**General Safety:**
- Enable 2FA (two-factor authentication) on your exchange account
- Keep small amounts in your "hot wallet" (daily use)
- Store large amounts in a hardware wallet when possible
- Don't leave your computer unattended while wallet is open
- Log out of your wallet when not in use

### Importing an Existing Wallet

If you already have a Stellar wallet from another service:

1. **Open Freighter**
   - Click the Freighter icon

2. **Select "Import Wallet"**
   - Click the "Import Wallet" option
   - This lets you import your existing wallet

3. **Enter Your Recovery Phrase**
   - Enter your 12-word recovery phrase
   - Make sure the words are in the correct order
   - Reference the original copy if needed

4. **Set a Password**
   - Create a new password for Freighter (can be different)
   - This password protects your wallet while in Freighter

5. **Verify Access**
   - You should see your accounts and balances
   - Verify the amounts match what you expect

## Troubleshooting

### I forgot my password

**If you remember your recovery phrase:**
1. Reinstall Freighter
2. Select "Import Wallet"
3. Enter your recovery phrase
4. Create a new password

**If you don't remember your recovery phrase:**
- Unfortunately, your funds are locked
- The recovery phrase cannot be reset
- Never share your recovery phrase with anyone claiming they can help

### I lost my recovery phrase

- Your funds are still safe
- You can continue using your wallet as long as you have your password
- Always write down your recovery phrase when you first create a wallet

### My wallet address changed

- Wallet addresses don't change
- You may be looking at a different account in Freighter
- Click on your account name to verify you're viewing the correct account

### My XLM hasn't arrived yet

- Stellar is usually very fast (5-10 seconds)
- Check the exchange to confirm the withdrawal was sent
- Your wallet address must be exact (even one character wrong will fail)
- Try refreshing your browser

### Can I have multiple wallets?

Yes! In Freighter, you can:
- Create multiple accounts within one wallet
- Import multiple wallets
- Switch between wallets easily

## Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Store your recovery phrase in a safe place
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable 2FA on exchanges
- Check website URLs before signing in
- Start small with amounts you can afford to lose

❌ **Don't:**
- Share your recovery phrase with anyone
- Use the same password across multiple services
- Store your recovery phrase digitally
- Click suspicious links
- Sign approvals you don't understand
