# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is Stellar Tip Jar?

Stellar Tip Jar is a decentralized tipping platform built on the Stellar blockchain. It allows supporters to send cryptocurrency tips to creators instantly and securely, without intermediaries. Creators can receive tips for their work, whether that's music, art, content creation, software, or any other creative endeavor.

### How does it work?

1. Creators create a profile and share their link
2. Supporters visit the profile and connect their wallet
3. Supporters send tips in XLM (Stellar Lumens)
4. Tips appear in the creator's account in real-time
5. Creators can withdraw tips to their wallet anytime

### Is it free to use?

Stellar Tip Jar is mostly free:
- **Free for supporters:** No fees to send tips
- **Free for creators:** No account creation fees
- **Small platform fee:** Small percentage on each tip (typically 2-5%)
- **Network fee:** Negligible Stellar network fee (~0.00001 XLM)

Your total cost = Tip Amount + Platform Fee + Network Fee

### What cryptocurrencies are supported?

Currently, Stellar Tip Jar supports **XLM (Stellar Lumens)** exclusively. This is the native currency of the Stellar network and enables fast, cheap transactions.

### Is Stellar Tip Jar safe?

Yes! Stellar Tip Jar uses:
- Blockchain security (Stellar network)
- Decentralized transactions
- No central server storing private keys
- Only you control your wallet
- Open-source code (likely audited)

See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for more details.

### What is the Stellar network?

Stellar is a blockchain (cryptocurrency network) designed for fast, secure payments. It offers:
- Fast transactions (5 seconds)
- Low fees (fractions of a cent)
- Decentralized (no server failures)
- Open source
- Established since 2014

Learn more at [GLOSSARY.md](GLOSSARY.md).

## For Supporters

### How do I send a tip?

Quick overview:
1. Set up a Freighter wallet and add XLM
2. Go to a creator's profile on Stellar Tip Jar
3. Enter your tip amount
4. Click "Send Tip"
5. Approve in your wallet
6. Done! Tip sent in seconds

See [SENDING_TIPS.md](SENDING_TIPS.md) for detailed steps.

### What are the fees?

Fees vary but typically include:
- **Stellar Network Fee:** ~0.00001 XLM (negligible)
- **Platform Fee:** Usually 2-5% of tip amount
- Example: 10 XLM tip might cost 10.5 XLM total

Check the amount displayed before confirming.

### Can I get a refund?

Blockchain transactions are final and irreversible:
- You cannot undo a confirmed transaction
- Always double-check before confirming
- Make sure amount and recipient are correct

If you send to the wrong person, you'd need to ask them to return it (unlikely). Be careful!

### Is my tip anonymous?

Tips are **semi-anonymous:**
- Creators see your wallet address
- Your name might show if your wallet has a display name set
- Message attached to your tip is visible
- Previous tips you've sent are visible in transaction history
- If you add a message, the creator knows it's from you

You can manage this in your wallet settings.

### Do I have to add a message?

No, messages are optional:
- You can tip without any message
- Messages help creators know you appreciated them
- Nice messages are usually appreciated!
- Keep messages friendly and respectful

### What if my tip doesn't go through?

If a tip fails:
1. Your wallet didn't approve it
2. Check your wallet for error message
3. Ensure you have enough XLM balance
4. Try again or contact support

Your balance won't change if rejected.

### Can someone tip me without setting up crypto first?

No, supporters need:
- A Stellar wallet (like Freighter)
- XLM in their wallet
- Basic blockchain understanding

This is a requirement of the Stellar platform.

### Do I have to use Freighter wallet?

No, any Stellar-compatible wallet works:
- Freighter (recommended, easiest)
- Albedo
- xBull
- Lobstr
- Others

See [WALLET_SETUP.md](WALLET_SETUP.md) for alternatives.

## For Creators

### How do I become a creator?

Steps:
1. Connect your Freighter wallet to Stellar Tip Jar
2. Click "Create Creator Profile"
3. Choose a username
4. Add profile info (bio, picture, links)
5. Share your profile link

See [GETTING_STARTED.md](GETTING_STARTED.md) and [PROFILE_SETUP.md](PROFILE_SETUP.md).

### How do I share my profile?

Copy your profile link and:
- Add to your social media bio
- Share in Discord communities
- Mention in YouTube descriptions
- Post on Twitter/Instagram
- Add to your website

See [RECEIVING_TIPS.md](RECEIVING_TIPS.md#sharing-your-profile).

### When can I withdraw my tips?

You can withdraw anytime:
1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click "Withdraw"
3. Confirm in your wallet
4. Tips arrive in 5-20 seconds

No minimum withdrawal amount (except Stellar's 1 XLM minimum balance).

### Are there fees for withdrawing?

Minimal:
- **Platform withdrawal fee:** Usually minimal or free
- **Network fee:** ~0.00001 XLM (negligible)
- Check the amount displayed before confirming

### How do I verify my profile?

Verification features vary:
- Some platforms have verification badges
- Usually requires proof of identity
- Contact support for verification options
- Not required to receive tips

### Do I need to pay taxes on tips?

**This depends on your location.** Tips might be taxable income:
- Check with a tax professional
- Different countries have different rules
- You may need to report all crypto income
- Conversion to fiat might affect taxes

This is not financial advice.

### How do I convert XLM to cash?

1. Sign up for an exchange (Kraken, Coinbase, etc.)
2. Create account and verify identity
3. Withdraw your XLM from Stellar Tip Jar to exchange
4. Sell XLM for USD/EUR/etc.
5. Withdraw to your bank account

Takes 1-5 business days typically.

### Can I have multiple profiles?

Yes, but with limitations:
- Create multiple accounts with different wallets
- One profile per wallet per platform
- Use the same wallet for related profiles
- Combine identities if similar

### How do I delete my profile?

Contact support to request profile deletion:
- May be able to keep earnings
- Tips received before deletion might remain
- Your username may become available
- Check support documentation for process

## Technical Questions

### What is a blockchain?

A blockchain is a ledger (database) that:
- Records all transactions
- No single server controls it
- Distributed across thousands of computers
- Secure (each block is cryptographically linked)
- Transparent (anyone can verify)
- Immutable (can't be changed)

### Why blockchain for tipping?

Blockchain enables:
- No middleman (lower fees)
- Fast transactions (seconds, not days)
- Global payments (no borders)
- Decentralized (no single point of failure)
- Transparent (verify transactions yourself)
- User control (only you have your keys)

### What is XLM?

XLM (Stellar Lumen) is:
- The native currency of Stellar
- Used for tipping on this platform
- Relatively stable cryptocurrency
- Can be traded for other cryptocurrencies or fiat

### Why is my transaction pending?

Usually just confirmation:
- Stellar is fast (5-10 seconds typically)
- Refresh the page after a few seconds
- Check your wallet for transaction status
- Contact support if pending 2+ minutes

### What if I lose access to my wallet?

**If you have your recovery phrase:**
- Create a new instance of the wallet
- Import using recovery phrase
- Set a new password
- Access all your funds

**If you lost your recovery phrase:**
- Your funds are permanently inaccessible
- The recovery phrase cannot be reset
- **Always save your recovery phrase!**

See [WALLET_SETUP.md](WALLET_SETUP.md) for prevention.

### Is my data secure?

Stellar Tip Jar protects your data:
- Uses standard encryption
- Your private keys never leave your wallet
- Platform doesn't store sensitive data
- Regular security audits (likely)

But nothing is 100% secure:
- Enable 2FA where available
- Use strong passwords
- Never share private keys
- Keep your device secure

### What is a private key / recovery phrase?

**Recovery phrase (seed):**
- 12-24 words that recreate your wallet
- Never share with anyone
- Store safely offline
- Can recover all funds with this

**Private key:**
- Long string that proves wallet ownership
- Gives access to all funds
- Never share
- Same security as recovery phrase

### How are transactions confirmed?

Stellar network:
1. You sign transaction with your wallet
2. Transaction broadcast to network
3. Validators verify the transaction
4. Transaction added to ledger
5. Confirmation appears (usually 5 seconds)

### Can someone steal my tips?

Only if:
- They get your recovery phrase (guard it!)
- They get your password (use strong password)
- You approve malicious transaction (verify first!)
- Your device is compromised (keep it secure)

Otherwise, your crypto is secure.

## Troubleshooting

### My wallet won't connect

**Solution:**
- Ensure Freighter is installed
- Make sure wallet is unlocked
- Refresh the page
- Try incognito window
- Restart browser
- Check your internet connection

See [TROUBLESHOOTING.md](TROUBLESHOOTING.md) for more.

### Transaction failed

**Likely causes:**
- Insufficient balance
- Wallet rejected it
- Network issue
- Invalid recipient

**Solution:**
- Check your balance
- Try again
- Contact support with error message

### Can't find a creator

**Try:**
- Use the search bar
- Check spelling of username
- Browse by category
- Check social media for their link
- Contact creator directly

### My tip didn't send

**Check:**
- Did wallet pop up? (no = try again or check balance)
- Did you approve it? (if no, tip didn't send)
- Wait 30 seconds and refresh
- Check your wallet transaction history
- Contact support if stuck
