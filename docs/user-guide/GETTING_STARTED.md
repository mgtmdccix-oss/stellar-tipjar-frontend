# Getting Started with Stellar Tip Jar

Welcome to Stellar Tip Jar! This guide will help you get started whether you're a supporter looking to tip creators or a creator ready to receive tips.

## What is Stellar Tip Jar?

Stellar Tip Jar is a platform that allows you to support your favorite creators using cryptocurrency on the Stellar network. It's a fast, secure, and decentralized way to send and receive tips without intermediaries.

---

## For Supporters

Supporters are users who want to send tips to their favorite creators. Follow these steps to get started:

### Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

Before you can send tips, you'll need a Stellar wallet to hold your cryptocurrency.

1. **Install Freighter Wallet Extension**
   - Visit [freighter.app](https://freighter.app)
   - Click "Add to Chrome" (or your browser)
   - Confirm the installation

2. **Create a New Wallet or Import Existing**
   - Click the Freighter icon in your browser
   - Select "Create New Wallet"
   - Store your recovery phrase safely (you'll need it if you lose access)
   - Set a strong password

3. **Fund Your Wallet with XLM**
   - Copy your wallet address from Freighter
   - Buy XLM (Stellar Lumens) from a cryptocurrency exchange
   - Withdraw XLM to your wallet address
   - Wait for confirmation (typically 5-10 seconds)

For detailed instructions, see [WALLET_SETUP.md](WALLET_SETUP.md).

### Step 2: Find Creators

Now that your wallet is ready, let's find creators to support!

1. **Visit the Explore Page**
   - Go to Stellar Tip Jar and click "Explore"

2. **Browse or Search for Creators**
   - Browse featured creators or use the search bar
   - Filter by category or creator type

3. **Click on a Creator Profile**
   - View their tip history and profile information
   - Read about what they do and why they accept tips

### Step 3: Send Your First Tip

You're all set! Now let's send your first tip:

1. **Enter Tip Amount**
   - Click the tip input field on the creator's profile
   - Enter the amount in XLM you'd like to send

2. **Add Optional Message**
   - (Optional) Add a personal message for the creator
   - This helps creators know who supported them!

3. **Click "Send Tip"**
   - Review the transaction details
   - Click the "Send Tip" button

4. **Confirm in Your Wallet**
   - Your wallet will pop up asking for confirmation
   - Review the transaction details and click "Approve"

5. **Wait for Confirmation**
   - The transaction usually completes in 5-10 seconds
   - You'll see a success message once complete

For more details, see [SENDING_TIPS.md](SENDING_TIPS.md).

## For Creators

Creators are users who receive tips from supporters. Follow these steps to start earning:

### Step 1: Create Your Profile

1. **Connect Your Wallet**
   - Visit Stellar Tip Jar
   - Click "Sign In" and connect your Freighter wallet
   - Approve the connection request

2. **Choose a Username**
   - Pick a unique, memorable username
   - This will be part of your profile URL

3. **Add Profile Information**
   - Add a bio describing what you do
   - Share links to your social media or website

4. **Upload Profile Picture**
   - Choose a clear, professional profile picture
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG

For detailed instructions, see [PROFILE_SETUP.md](PROFILE_SETUP.md).

### Step 2: Share Your Profile

Now let's tell people about your profile!

1. **Copy Your Profile Link**
   - Go to your profile page
   - Copy the URL from your browser's address bar

2. **Share on Social Media**
   - Add the link to your Instagram bio
   - Tweet your link to your followers
   - Share in Discord communities

3. **Add to Your Bio/Website**
   - Add your Tip Jar link to your Twitch bio
   - Include it in your YouTube channel description
   - Add it to your website or blog

### Step 3: Receive Tips

Congratulations! Tips will start coming in:

1. **Tips Appear in Real-Time**
   - See tips arrive instantly on your dashboard
   - View sender messages and tip amounts

2. **View Tip History**
   - Access your tip history to see all supporters
   - Track tips over time

3. **Withdraw to Your Wallet**
   - Go to your dashboard
   - Click "Withdraw Tips"
   - Confirm the transaction in your wallet
   - Tips are transferred to your wallet immediately

For more details, see [RECEIVING_TIPS.md](RECEIVING_TIPS.md).
