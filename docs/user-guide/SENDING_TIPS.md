# How to Send Tips

This guide walks you through sending tips to your favorite creators on Stellar Tip Jar.

## Prerequisites

Before you can send tips, make sure you have:
- A Freighter wallet installed (or compatible wallet)
- XLM in your wallet
- Connected to Stellar Tip Jar by signing in

If you need help setting up, see [GETTING_STARTED.md](GETTING_STARTED.md) or [WALLET_SETUP.md](WALLET_SETUP.md).

---

## Finding a Creator to Tip

### Using the Explore Page

1. **Navigate to Explore**
   - Click "Explore" in the main navigation
   - You'll see featured creators and categories

2. **Browse Creators**
   - Scroll through featured creators
   - Look for someone you want to support

3. **Search for a Creator**
   - Use the search bar at the top
   - Search by username or keywords
   - Filter by category (Artist, Musician, Developer, etc.)

4. **View Creator Profile**
   - Click on a creator's card
   - View their profile information and tip history
   - See recent tips and messages from supporters

### From a Creator's Website

Many creators link their Stellar Tip Jar profile on their:
- Website or blog
- Social media bio (Twitter, Instagram, etc.)
- YouTube channel description
- Twitch profile

Just click the link to go directly to their profile.

## Sending Your Tip

Let's walk through the complete tipping process:

### Step 1: Open the Tip Form

1. **Go to Creator's Profile**
   - Navigate to the creator you want to tip
   - This can be from the Explore page or a direct link

2. **Locate the Tip Input**
   - Look for "Send Tip" or tip amount input on the profile
   - This is usually prominently displayed

### Step 2: Enter Tip Amount

1. **Enter Your Tip Amount**
   - Click the amount input field
   - Type the number of XLM (Stellar Lumens) you want to send
   - Example: 5 (for 5 XLM)

2. **Understand Transaction Fees**
   - Stellar network fee: ~0.00001 XLM (negligible)
   - Stellar Tip Jar fee: Variable (displayed before you send)
   - Your total cost = Tip Amount + Fees
   - Check the total before confirming

3. **Choose Tip Amount Wisely**
   - Minimum tip: 1 XLM (Stellar minimum balance)
   - No maximum limit
   - Think about what feels right to you
   - You can always tip again later

**Example Tip Amounts:**
- $1 USD ≈ 0.1 XLM
- $5 USD ≈ 0.5 XLM
- $10 USD ≈ 1-2 XLM

(XLM price varies, check current rate at your exchange)

### Step 3: Add a Message (Optional)

1. **Click the Message Field**
   - Below the amount, you may see "Add Message" or similar

2. **Write Your Message**
   - Keep it friendly and concise
   - Limit: usually 256 characters
   - Examples:
     - "Love your content!"
     - "Your latest video was amazing!"
     - "Keep up the great work!"

3. **Leave Message Blank (Optional)**
   - You don't have to include a message
   - Just click "Send Tip" to continue

### Step 4: Review Transaction Details

1. **Check the Summary**
   - Verify the creator name
   - Confirm the tip amount
   - Check any message you added

2. **Verify Address**
   - Make sure the creator's wallet address is correct
   - The first and last few characters should be visible
   - Stellar addresses start with 'G'

### Step 5: Click "Send Tip"

1. **Click the Button**
   - Find and click "Send Tip" or similar button
   - You'll see a confirmation dialog

2. **Freighter Wallet Pop-up**
   - Your Freighter wallet will ask for approval
   - A window will pop up showing transaction details

### Step 6: Approve in Your Wallet

1. **Review Wallet Details**
   - Destination (creator's address): Check first few characters match
   - Amount: Verify the XLM amount
   - Memo: Check any message you added
   - Operations: Usually shows "Payment"

2. **Verify You Have Enough XLM**
   - Check your wallet balance in Freighter
   - You need: Tip Amount + Network Fee + Minimum Balance (1 XLM)
   - If insufficient, the wallet will show an error

3. **Click "Approve"**
   - Review everything one more time
   - Click the "Approve" or "Sign" button
   - Your transaction is now being submitted

### Step 7: Wait for Confirmation

1. **Watch for Confirmation**
   - Typical time: 5-10 seconds
   - Check the website for a success message
   - You may see a message like "Tip sent successfully!"

2. **After Confirmation**
   - The creator's tip counter increases
   - Your tip appears in their tip history
   - If you added a message, it's now visible to the creator

## After You Send a Tip

### Track Your Tip

1. **View in Creator's History**
   - Go back to the creator's profile
   - Scroll to tip history
   - Look for your tip and message

2. **Check Your Wallet**
   - Open Freighter
   - You should see the transaction in your history
   - Shows amount sent, timestamp, and recipient

### Send Another Tip

1. **Return to Creator's Profile**
   - Repeat the steps above to send another tip

2. **Send to a Different Creator**
   - Go to Explore
   - Find another creator
   - Follow the same steps

## Troubleshooting

### "Insufficient Balance" Error

**Problem:** You don't have enough XLM to cover the tip + fees.

**Solution:**
- Increase your wallet balance from an exchange
- Wait for a transaction to confirm
- Try a smaller tip amount
- See [WALLET_SETUP.md](WALLET_SETUP.md)

### Transaction Stuck Pending

**Problem:** Your transaction hasn't confirmed after 2+ minutes.

**Solution:**
- Refresh the page
- Check your Freighter history
- Refresh Freighter
- Try sending again with a new transaction
- Wait up to 5 minutes (very rare to take longer)

### Creator Address Invalid

**Problem:** You see an error about an invalid address.

**Solution:**
- This is usually a technical error on the platform
- Try refreshing the page
- Try sending to a different creator first
- Contact support if the problem persists

### My Tip Doesn't Show Up

**Problem:** You confirmed the transaction but don't see it on the creator's profile.

**Solution:**
- Wait 30 seconds and refresh the page
- The page may not update automatically
- Check your Freighter history to confirm it sent
- Contact support with your transaction hash

### Wallet Won't Connect

**Problem:** Can't sign in or wallet isn't connecting.

**Solution:**
- Make sure Freighter is installed and unlocked
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache
- Restart your browser
- Try a different browser
- See [TROUBLESHOOTING.md](TROUBLESHOOTING.md)

## Tips for a Good Experience

✅ **Do:**
- Start with a small amount to test
- Leave a nice message with your tip
- Double-check the creator name matches
- Make sure your wallet has funds before starting
- Verify your internet connection is stable

❌ **Don't:**
- Send to an address you're not sure about
- Click suspicious links in creator bios
- Share your recovery phrase with anyone
- Assume high fees without checking first
- Send more than you can afford to lose

## Common Tip Etiquette

While completely optional, here are some friendly practices:

- **Small Creator:** 1-5 XLM is generous
- **Regular Support:** 5-10 XLM shows serious appreciation
- **Large Support:** 10+ XLM is a major contribution
- **Special Occasion:** Tip extra if celebrating artist milestone
- **Encouraging Message:** A kind comment means a lot

Remember, any tip size is appreciated! It's about showing support, not the amount.
